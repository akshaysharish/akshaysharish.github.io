Currently, I am pursuing a PhD at BITS-Pilani, Goa. I work in the field of Nonlinear Dynamics and complex systems. I use reduced neuron models (simplified versions of models describing the behaviour of neurons) to study emergent phenomena when there is a network of a large number of neurons. 

This is a place to keep the resources as organised as possible. It’s sort of a digital diary that I intend to use for keeping notes of the things that interest me, things I am supposed to (and/or interested in) learn, and the things that distract me for a great duration and keep me from doing that. 

I would be more than happy if anyone other than myself finds it useful.
