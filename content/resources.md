### Resources related to Nonlinear dynamics and chaos:

#### Some of the books I found useful while starting the subject.
1. *Nonlinear dynamics and chaos: With applications to Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Engineering*
   * By Steven H Strogatz
2. *Nonlinear Ordinary Differential Equations*
   * By R Grimshaw
3. *Chaos in dynamical systems*
    * By Edward Ott 
4. *Chaos: An introduction to dynamical systems*
    * By Kathleen Alligood, Tim Sauer, James Yorke
